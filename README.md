# SustainIQ - Your Green Impact Hub 🌍

**Live App Demo:** [sustainiq-bjq.caffeine.xyz](https://sustainiq-bjq.caffeine.xyz/)

SustainIQ is an all-in-one ecosystem designed to make sustainable living intuitive, interactive, and rewarding. The platform empowers users to track their carbon footprints, discover hidden energy waste, gamify eco-friendly habits, and visualize positive environmental impacts in real-time.

Built as an anonymous local-state MVP, SustainIQ combines quick, friction-free onboarding with high-fidelity simulations to drive real community awareness.

---

## ✨ Core Features

*   **Interactive Carbon Footprint Calculator:** Input your daily habits and receive instantaneous, real-time CO₂ output estimations.
*   **Eco-Financial ROI Simulator:** Visually track the alignment between sustainability and personal finance, showing exactly how much money you save as your CO₂ reductions grow.
*   **Reverse-Cart E-Commerce Simulator:** Paste or type items into your shopping cart to run a free-text parser that analyzes your carbon footprint and suggests eco-friendly alternatives.
*   **Vampire Power Finder:** Spot and eliminate the stealthy, phantom electricity drains drawing power from your home appliances when they aren't in use.
*   **Dynamic Virtual Forest:** Fund carbon offsets via a simulated checkout experience and watch your personal digital woodland dynamically grow over time.
*   **21-Day Habit-Loop Challenges:** Gamified sustainability sprints with automated, local streak tracking to lock in eco-friendly daily routines.
*   **Green Infrastructure Map:** An interactive localized directory helping you find nearby recycling centers, composting points, and green resources.
*   **Eco-Score Badges & One-Click Social Kit:** Showcase your environmental milestones with styled on-screen achievement badges and instant text sharing snippets.

## 🛠️ Architecture & Tech Stack

SustainIQ is a full-stack decentralized application built entirely using the **Caffeine.ai** self-writing engineering framework:

*   **Backend Runtime:** Native [Motoko](https://internetcomputer.org/) smart contracts, optimizing linguistic reasoning patterns and mathematical consistency for complex data formulas.
*   **Hosting & Decentralization:** Deployed fully on-chain directly via the **Internet Computer Protocol (ICP)** global network layer—guaranteeing 100% tamperproof execution, data protection, and permanent availability.
*   **State Management:** State persistence leveraging localized browser storage (`localStorage`) and sovereign on-chain states ensuring a zero-friction, non-siloed layout.
*   **Frontend UI Layer:** Responsive, fully adaptive layouts styled with a fluid modern CSS system, optimizing layout delivery across mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.
